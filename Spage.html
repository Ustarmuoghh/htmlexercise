Frontend Project: Basic HTML

This simplie project focuses on HTML, Hypertext Mark-up Language, which is used to produce web pages.

In the following pages, you are given an example which tells you how to build a web page. What you need to do is to write you own web page by choosing a specific topic, for example about you, your pet, someone you know, or an imaginary person.

Note that whenever you go to school, you are going to learn and not to play around. Remember School is a place where you study, learn, probably earn one or two skills and earn a degree.

If you are not sure about the contents of your file, open it withNotepad on a Windows machine. If it is readable and looks like what you typed in, you should be fine.

Your web page must include following elements:

  • a file
  • at least two level headings
  • at least one paragraph
  • at least one list (ordered or unordered) with at least three list items
  • at least one hyperlink to another web page
  • at least one image
  • at least one of the images has a thumbnail
  • at least one horizontal rule, with author and modification date
  • at least one kind of normal text formatting (bold, italic or underline)
  • at least one table, with at least 3 columns and 3 rows
